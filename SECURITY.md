# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability, please email us at [security@funeducationtv.com](mailto:security@funeducationtv.com) with the details of the vulnerability. We will respond as quickly as possible to provide assistance.

## Steps for reporting:
1. Provide a clear description of the vulnerability.
2. Include any relevant details, such as steps to reproduce the issue.
3. Keep the communication private until we’ve had a chance to investigate and address the issue.

Thank you for helping us ensure the security of our project!
